Privacy Policy for SEO Pinterest App

Last updated: [May 25, 2025, e.g., May 25, 2025]

This Privacy Policy describes how the "SEO Pinterest App" (the "App") operates and collects, uses, and discloses information from its users.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

This App is a desktop application designed for local use and does not store any user data, personal information, API keys, or generated content on any external servers or databases, other than what is explicitly uploaded by the user to their own configured Google Cloud Storage bucket or sent to third-party APIs (Gemini, OpenAI, Pinterest) for processing as initiated by the user.

Your API keys (Gemini, OpenAI, Pinterest) are stored locally in your .env file and are never transmitted to us (the developers of this specific app).

2. Third-Party Services

This App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini API: Used for generating text content (titles, descriptions, etc.). Your prompts are sent to Gemini for processing.
  • OpenAI DALL-E API: Used for generating images based on textual descriptions. Your image prompts are sent to DALL-E for processing.
  • Google Cloud Storage: Used for storing generated images in a bucket you control. Images are uploaded directly from your local machine to your GCS bucket.
  • Pinterest API: (To be implemented) Used for posting pins to your Pinterest account. Pin details (image URL, title, description, alt text, target URL) will be sent to Pinterest.

Please refer to the privacy policies of these third-party services for information on how they handle data:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy
  • Pinterest Privacy Policy: https://policy.pinterest.com/privacy-policy

3. Data Handling and Storage

All processing of user inputs, generation of content, and storage of API keys happens locally on the user's computer or directly through the user's configured third-party cloud services. No data is stored or processed by the developers of this app.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
